Hourly - Documentation

Welcome to Hourly! This extension eliminates the hassle of having to enter in your time manually each week for your on-campus job.

Enter Hours
Select the hours you generally work each week.
Save Changes
Press the save changes button.
Apply Hours
When you're ready to fill in your hours, just click apply!

As you can tell, it is quite easy. If your hours don't change much during the semester, this extension is great for you! When you're ready to enter your hours for the week, just click apply and your hours will be filled into Student Link for you automatically.
